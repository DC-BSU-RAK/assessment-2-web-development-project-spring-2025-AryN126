Parent Essentials at Repton Dubai

At Repton Dubai, we understand that a student’s academic success and emotional well-being are closely linked to strong, supportive partnerships between school and home. Our Parent Essentials hub is designed to be a one-stop resource—providing all the key information parents need to stay informed, organized, and engaged throughout their child’s educational journey.

We are committed to creating a transparent and collaborative environment where every parent feels heard, empowered, and connected to their child’s school experience.

Academic Calendars & School Timings

Stay up-to-date with important dates and schedules:

  • Academic calendars with key term dates, holidays, and exam periods.
  • Daily school hours for each year group, including drop-off and pick-up times.
  • Special event dates including open days, performances, sports days, and parent-teacher conferences.

Parents can access this information anytime through our online portal or the school app.

Uniform Guidelines

At Repton Dubai, our uniform reflects our school values—pride, respect, and unity. Our uniform policy ensures students maintain a smart and consistent appearance, while also being comfortable and confident in their attire.

  • Detailed uniform checklists by age group.
  • Seasonal uniform guidelines (summer/winter options).
  • PE and sportswear requirements.
  • Approved uniform suppliers and purchase locations.

We also provide support for new families to help with fittings and first-time uniform orders.

Transport Services

Our safe and reliable school transport service offers convenient travel to and from campus, covering a wide range of residential areas across Dubai.

  • Air-conditioned, GPS-tracked buses with trained staff.
  • Designated pick-up and drop-off points.
  • Real-time updates and alerts for delays or route changes.
  • Strict safety protocols and regular vehicle maintenance.

Parents receive transport handbooks, route maps, and contact information for the transport coordinator.

Meal Plans and Dining Options

Nutritious meals are an essential part of student health and learning. We offer:

  • Cafeteria services with a variety of freshly prepared, balanced meal options.
  • Age-appropriate menus designed by nutritionists.
  • Allergy and dietary accommodations.
  • Prepaid meal cards or online payment systems for easy management.

Parents can access weekly menus, dietary information, and food policies through the parent portal.

School Policies & Guidelines

Transparency is key to trust. Repton Dubai provides full access to all essential school policies, including:

  • Safeguarding & Child Protection
  • Health and Safety Guidelines
  • Attendance & Absence Protocols
  • Behaviour and Discipline Policy
  • Digital Device & Internet Usage Policy

All policies are reviewed regularly and made accessible via the website or school app.

Communication & Contacts

Open communication is at the heart of our parent-school relationship. Whether you need to discuss academic progress, well-being, or administrative matters, we ensure that support is just a message or call away.

  • Direct contact information for class teachers and tutors
  • Heads of Year and Department
  • Admissions and administrative offices
  • Health and safety teams
  • Parent-Teacher Conferences scheduled throughout the year
  • Real-time updates through the parent communication platform, mobile notifications, newsletters, and digital bulletins

Parent Involvement & Community Engagement

Repton Dubai believes in creating a vibrant and involved school community. Parents are encouraged to participate in:

  • Workshops and seminars on parenting, digital safety, and academic support
  • Cultural celebrations, performances, and sports events
  • The Parent-Teacher Association (PTA), which provides opportunities for feedback, collaboration, and volunteering

Your voice matters, and we strive to make you an active part of your child’s educational experience.

Digital Access: All in One Place

We understand the pace of modern family life. That’s why we provide all Parent Essentials through a centralized digital platform, accessible via desktop and mobile:

  • View and download calendars, menus, and policies
  • Communicate directly with teachers and staff
  • Track transport and attendance
  • Manage school fees and uniform orders
  • Receive updates and alerts instantly

We’re Here for You

Our commitment to parents goes beyond providing information—we offer partnership, support, and trust. Whether you're joining Repton Dubai for the first time or have been part of our family for years, we are here to make your experience smooth and meaningful.

Need Help or Have Questions?

  • Contact our Parent Support Team
  • Email us at support@reptondubai.org
  • Visit the Parent Reception Desk during school hours

Your journey with Repton is a shared one. Together, we build a community where every child—and every parent—feels supported, informed, and empowered.
