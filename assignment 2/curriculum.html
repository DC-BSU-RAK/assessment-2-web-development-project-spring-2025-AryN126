Curriculum at Repton Dubai

At Repton Dubai, our curriculum is more than a framework for academic success — it is a foundation for lifelong learning, character development, and global readiness. Rooted in the UK National Curriculum, our academic programme reflects the best of British education while embracing the diverse needs of our international student community.

A Seamless Journey from Early Years to Sixth Form

Our curriculum supports students through each stage of development, from the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) to Sixth Form, ensuring smooth academic progression with well-defined goals:

  • Early Years: Play-based learning focused on early literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional development.
  • Primary Years: Builds core skills in English, Mathematics, Science, and a broad range of subjects including arts and physical education.
  • Middle School: Subject-specialist teaching and increased structure encourage independence and deeper academic thinking.
  • Senior School: Offers IGCSE, A-Level, and IB Diploma Programme (IBDP), allowing students to personalise their academic pathways.

Globally Recognised Pathways: IGCSE, A-Level & IB Diploma

In the senior years, students benefit from flexible qualification options that open doors to top universities worldwide:

  • IGCSE: A respected qualification providing academic discipline and strong preparation for future study.
  • A-Levels: Allows focused, in-depth study of subjects aligned with university goals.
  • IBDP: A holistic programme balancing academic rigor with global awareness through core components like TOK, EE, and CAS.

A Curriculum for the 21st Century

Repton Dubai embraces educational innovation to prepare students for the modern world:

  • Digital tools that promote collaborative learning and research
  • STEM-focused education that fosters innovation
  • Project-based learning tackling real-world challenges

Key competencies include critical thinking, collaboration, communication, digital literacy, and global awareness—skills essential for future success.

Beyond the Classroom: A Holistic Education

Our curriculum develops the whole child across intellectual, emotional, social, and ethical dimensions:

  • Values Education: Promotes integrity, respect, and responsibility rooted in UAE and British values.
  • Creative Arts: Encourages self-expression through art, music, drama, and design.
  • Physical Education: Fosters fitness, teamwork, and perseverance through diverse sports programmes.
  • Global Citizenship: Initiatives like Model UN and service projects build empathy and international mindedness.

Learning Support and Differentiation

We personalise learning to meet every student’s needs:

  • Support programmes for students with additional needs
  • English as an Additional Language (EAL) for non-native speakers
  • Gifted and talented pathways for advanced learners
  • Differentiated instruction in every classroom

Teacher Excellence and Continuous Innovation

Our outstanding faculty is key to our success. Teachers at Repton Dubai bring:

  • Strong academic credentials
  • Passion for inspiring and engaging learners
  • Commitment to ongoing professional development

We foster a reflective and forward-thinking teaching culture that keeps our curriculum relevant and impactful.

University and Career Preparation

We prepare students for life after school through:

  • Early and ongoing university and career guidance
  • Mentorships, internships, and leadership opportunities
  • Proven success in admissions to top global universities like Oxford, Cambridge, Harvard, and Stanford

A Foundation for Life

At Repton Dubai, our curriculum is more than just academic content—it’s a platform for developing future leaders who are confident, compassionate, and globally aware. We prepare students not just to succeed in school, but to thrive in life.
