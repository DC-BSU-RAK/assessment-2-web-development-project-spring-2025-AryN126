Welcome to Repton Dubai

Repton Dubai is a world-class British school offering exceptional academic programs, holistic development, and a nurturing environment that allows each student to thrive. Located in the heart of Dubai, we embrace a diverse, inclusive, and globally-minded community where every child is given the tools they need to succeed in school and life.

Our students embark on a transformative educational journey that prepares them to be confident, compassionate, and capable leaders in a fast-changing world.

Join us at Repton Dubai, where academic excellence meets personal growth and global perspective.

Why Repton Dubai?

Choosing the right school is more than just academics—it's about finding an environment where your child will be nurtured, inspired, and empowered to reach their full potential. Here’s why families around the world trust Repton Dubai with their children's education:

A Truly International Experience

Our diverse community of students and staff represents over 80 nationalities, promoting a rich exchange of cultural perspectives and fostering mutual respect. We celebrate diversity and encourage global citizenship, ensuring that our students are prepared for success in an interconnected world.

Holistic Development

At Repton Dubai, we believe in educating the whole child. Beyond academics, we offer a wide range of co-curricular activities, including music, drama, arts, sports, and leadership programs. These activities provide students with opportunities to develop essential life skills such as creativity, teamwork, and resilience.

World-Class Faculty

Our teachers are passionate professionals who are committed to fostering an environment of academic excellence and personal growth. With experience from top schools and universities worldwide, our faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Repton Dubai offers a modern campus with facilities designed to enhance learning and creativity. From cutting-edge science labs to performance theatres, swimming pools, and sports fields, our campus is built to inspire students and support their growth in every area.

Parent Essentials

We understand that being an informed and engaged parent is key to your child's success at Repton Dubai. Our Parent Essentials hub is designed to keep you updated on everything you need to know, including school policies, key dates, transport services, and more. Here’s what you’ll find:

Academic Calendars & School Timings

Stay up-to-date with the school year by accessing our academic calendar, which includes key term dates, holidays, exam schedules, and special event dates. You can easily check school timings for each year group, including drop-off and pick-up times, through our online portal.

Uniform Guidelines

Repton Dubai takes pride in maintaining a smart and consistent appearance among students. Our uniform policy ensures students are dressed in a way that reflects our core values of pride, respect, and unity. Detailed checklists for each age group, as well as seasonal guidelines and PE wear, are available for your convenience.

Transport Services

We offer a safe and reliable school transport service, with air-conditioned buses covering a wide range of residential areas in Dubai. The buses are GPS-tracked, and parents receive real-time updates about delays and route changes.

Meal Plans & Dining Options

Our on-site cafeteria offers a variety of healthy, balanced meals, with menus designed by nutritionists. We also accommodate special dietary needs and allergies. Parents can access weekly menus and dietary information through the parent portal, ensuring their child receives nutritious meals throughout the school day.

Admissions

We’re excited that you’re considering joining the Repton Dubai family! Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that your child’s journey with us begins smoothly. Here’s what you can expect:

How to Apply

The Repton Dubai admissions process is simple and user-friendly. You can apply directly through our online application portal, where you’ll find all the necessary forms and instructions. We accept students from a wide range of age groups, from Early Years to Sixth Form.

Our Admissions Team

Our dedicated admissions team is here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you have questions about the curriculum, school facilities, or the application process, we’re available to provide you with all the information you need.

Open Days & School Tours

We invite you to book a tour of our campus to see firsthand what makes Repton Dubai such a unique educational experience. During these tours, you’ll have the opportunity to meet our faculty, tour our facilities, and get a feel for the Repton community.
